Sepulchral Inscription of Flavia Faustina

Edited by: Tom Elliott

Provenance:

Text

Flavia Faustina vixit an-
nos duos mensis octo ⌜e⌝t
diis octo.

Translation

Flavia Faustina lived two years, eight months and eight days.
